Domestic Violence

Welcome to my website

Domestic Violence is an issue that occurs on a daily basis. It's something that the society assumes "normal" in relationships and something that should be kept as a secret in homes. This website's purpose is to make everyone aware of how mentaly toxic and physically exhausting it is to be going through Doemstic Violence and why it needs to be known!

"The effects of abuse are devastating and far-reaching. Domestic Violence speaks many languages, has many colours and many different communities."

-Sandra Pupatello

Family,Friends,Adults,Children, Old people etc. Unfortunetly there just isn't an age limit for Domestic Violence to happen in homes. Hence why it needs to be stopped. The only way we can do this if we mordenize our community and the society. Educate them how important it is to know how deadly domestic violence can be and that opening up about it is not a way to bring shame or disgrace to their reputations.
